# An AI based project 
  Game: Rock, Paper, Scissors [AI BASED ]
## This is not just a random game. It has a strategical Master Mind of it's own.
  This is a Python-based Rock, Paper, SCissors Game that learns from the user's moves and analyzes every move 

  ## How it works:
This game doesn't just play randomly, It learns from the user history of moves, and strategically palns it's next move, it adapts to the play Using Three Logical Levels:

** THE COUNTER: It uses a Counter variable to count the specific moves of the user. And Remembers your last move.

** THE STRATEGIST: It checks your history, to find your favourite move and tries to tackle it.

** THE MIRROR: It plays the mirroe opposite of your last move.
